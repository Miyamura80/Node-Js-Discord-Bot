**Quinn the armless**

A drunken martial arts master, first seen in the taverns of [Glimmerdrift]. 
Quinn lost his arms 10 years ago, and learned "Drunken Arts of the legs".

__*Combat:*__

Quinn fights with his legs, through a series of kicking attacks. His signature kicks involve him using his often drunken state to increase randomness of effectiveness, but increase the upper bound of his damage output.  

__*Allegiance:*__

Part of the way of the hand.
